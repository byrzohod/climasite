## Context

What is the problem? What forces are at play (technical, organizational, user-facing)? What constraints are non-negotiable? Keep this section focused on *what's true before the decision* — no solutioning yet.

## Decision

The single decision, stated as a direct sentence. Then, if the decision has sub-parts, break them out:

### 1. Sub-decision — **Chosen option**

Rationale: why this option beats the alternatives, in the specific context above.

**Rejected:** briefly name each rejected alternative and the *specific reason* it lost. Future readers need to know what was considered and why it was dropped, not just what was picked.

## Consequences

**Positive:** what becomes easier, cheaper, safer, or possible that wasn't before.

**Negative / risks:** what becomes harder, more expensive, or newly fragile. What assumptions could invalidate this decision later? What's the tripwire that would make us reopen this ADR?
